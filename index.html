Welcome to the Avengers Universe!

Avengers Origin

A man named Nick Fury scouted a group of superheroes known as The Avengers! These heroes come together to save the world from threats too big for any one hero to tackle alone.

Fun Fact!

The Avengers are often called the "World's Mightiest Heroes!" They protect the Earth from all sorts of dangers, from aliens to time-traveling villains.

The Avengers' Team

The Avengers are a team of iconic superheroes:

  • Iron Man: Genius inventor and billionaire playboy.
  • Captain America: Super-soldier with an unbreakable shield.
  • Thor: The God of Thunder.
  • Hulk: Scientist turned green powerhouse.
  • Black Widow: Expert spy and hand-to-hand combatant.
  • Hawkeye: Master marksman with a bow and arrow.
