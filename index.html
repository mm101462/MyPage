The Guardians of the Galaxy are like the ultimate team of misfits who somehow end up saving the universe. There’s Star-Lord, the cool guy who thinks he’s always the hero, Rocket the talking raccoon who’s super smart and loves explosions, Groot who’s a tree but can only say “I am Groot,” Drax who’s tough and serious but doesn’t get jokes, and Gamora, the badass warrior who used to be a villain but now fights for the good guys. They’re always arguing and messing around, but when they need to, they pull together and kick butt. They're funny, awesome, and no one else could really pull off being that weird and still save the day!
